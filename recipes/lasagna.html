Lasagna



Description

Layers of red sauce, noodles, cheese, and meat

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown meat
  2. Add tomato sauce and seasoning
  3. Cook noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  5. In a baking dish, stack laters of noodles, meat sauce, and cheese
  6. Bake for 50 minutes
